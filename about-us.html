About Us

The collection aims to create truly circular products through a process that involves traceability and transparency. Each product in the collection comes with a Digital Product Passport through a QR code, providing full transparency of the whole lifecycle of the garment, including information on the manufacturing process, environmental impact, and guidance on repair, reuse, up-cycling, and recycling.
